2/24/17 5:50 BAG 'N' TAG FUNDRAISER

In 40 minutes, we will be open at the Downtown Tacoma Umpqua Bank for our annual fundraiser! I woul dlike to personally thank all of our sponsors and guests who will attend this Bag 'N' Tag fundraiser!

At the fundraiser, we've been hard at work setting up a live and silent auction, refreshments, a food truck, a jewelry table, rice crispy treat robot station, and much more! Please enjoy yourselves, and join us if you can!


2/21/17 1:10 FINAL AFTERNOON OF BUILD SEASON

We now have less than 8 hours to finish the robot, and everyone is kicking into maximum overdrive! Programming is still working on vision through the RasberryPi, and will move onto fine-tuning driver controls later this afternoon.

Media, PR and Awards are steadily prepping for our Bag 'N' Tag celebration on Friday. In addition to that, that team is also working on our Chairman's video.

Outreach team has just left to work with students at a local elementary school.
Build and CAD team are working on finalizing wiring on the bot. We can't wait to see our robot finished by our hard working build team!


2/20/17 2:41 COUNTDOWN TO BAG 'N' TAG

With one day and hours left to Bag 'N' Tag, the build team and programming team are hard at work putting the finishing touches on our robot! Our programmers have been working with vision processing code for auto alignment during shooting with a RaspberryPi. Our Media, PR, and Outreach teams are working together to arrange our annual Bag 'N' Tag fundraiser.

03/25/2017 8:29 am GLACIER PEAK: STANDS - SET UP AND READY TO GO

Our team unloaded the shop (at the pit) yesterday here at GlacierPeak. Our
robot is doing great and improved alot over these passed few weeks. I'm so excited
for the competition to start up again.

Our team set up in the near center of the stands, however I am set up at a corner,
sitting with Apex only to plug in the laptop. I can see alot of people moving
outand in of the stands (maybe to check up on their team's robot), I kind of understand,
sitting on rough seats for hours straight can be tough. I can hear lots of people
chatting in groups, followed by the sounds of the buzzer when it reaches
specific points of time in the game, however, its just the volenteers testing them
out to see if the game is set up and working.

3/25/2017 12:03 pm GLACIER PEAK: STANDS/CAFETERIA - STARTED OUT WITH A GREAT START

Oh yupp! Our team is doing great, and our alliance worked so well together, thank you 2605,
and 1318. We won our first match (of the Glacier Peak event 2017). The drive team brought the robot
down to the pit for the pit crew to work on the vision system (the vision system is
mostly a camera mounted onto the robot to help the drive team see where/how to collect gears),
and a guide for the shooter. Meanwhile the documentary people ended up talking to the drive team
right after the match, followed by the judges talking to our pit crew about the robot. They also
send some of the drive team to other teams that they were paired up with in the next match.

Later,I went to the scouting crew (the people who stay in the stands and keep track of other teams
robots (scouting is a big deal!) to see how they were doing. They said the new scouting system is
doing great. Alex (development captain) made a code or a program that will improve the scouting system.

Sponsorship Tiers

To determine what sponsors go on our T-shirts, robots, or banners, we have formulated different tiers based on the monetary value of the sponsorship or how much money the sponsor donates. All tiers will receive a thank you letter with a picture of our team and completed robot, at the end of competition season.

Diamond Level- $3,000+

Your company name will be featured in our official team name, in addition to all previously mentioned benefits.

Platinum Level- $2,000-$2,999

Your company logo will be featured on our completed robot, in addition to all previously mentioned benefits.

Gold Level- $1000-$1,999

Your company logo will be featured on our team banner, used at competitions, in addition to all previously mentioned benefits.

Silver Level- $500-$999

Your company logo will be featured on our team’s t-shirts, in addition to Bronze Level benefits.

Bronze Level- $100-$499

Your company logo will be featured on our brochure and website.
